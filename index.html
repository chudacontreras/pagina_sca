Supply Chain Architects provide professional logistics and supply chain solutions and services for various industries:

RETAIL

SCA provides excellent supply chain services for retail and e-commerce businesses in the UK and throughout the EU. Our customers benefit from a full range of services, including freight, offshore processing and distribution, customs compliance, and order fulfilment and management solutions. Our logistics solutions and 24/7 support are ideal for consumer brand manufacturers and retailers, which means you can concentrate on your retail business.

FASHION

Fashion is the definitive fast-paced consumer industry. The demand for new trends and fashions on the high street requires perfect timing, accurate order management, and high reliability for designers, manufacturers, distributors and retailers. Let SCA organise your product flow, from the cat-walk to the high street.

AEROSPACE

SCA provides outstanding supply chain services and order management systems to global civil and military aviation manufacturers. Our sophisticated management solutions, a worldwide network of logistics partners, and experienced freight experts can help you manage small or large engineering components in any location and within any deadline; all of these are governed by our comprehensive 24/7 tracking and order management tools provide support.

TECHNOLOGY

Maintaining contract deadlines and minimising machine downtime is critical for high-tech manufacturers. SCA provides flexible, customised supply chain solutions for manufacturers in the high-tech engineering and professional consumer products sectors in the UK and internationally. Services range from experts handling test equipment or fragile parts to fully integrated purchase orders and fulfilment management solutions to help resolve customs compliance and customs clearance issues.

AUTOMOTIVE

SCA provides first-class supply chain services and order management systems to automotive and engineering manufacturers in the UK and abroad. Our customised management solutions, a network of logistics partners and facilities all over the world can help you manage the logistics of small or large goods to and from any location and almost any deadline. Our comprehensive 24/7 tracking and order management tools support all supply chain services.

MANUFACTURING

SCA provides customised solutions and professional supply chain services to support many organisations in industry and manufacturing. Our expertise in managing the international transportation of heavy machinery, our experience in controlling the logistics of critical parts, and our plans to ensure customs compliance help optimise plant performance, minimise order management, and maximise the value of product flow.

TELECOMS

The telecommunications industry is highly competitive and requires efficient and flexible supply chain solutions to meet professional needs. SCA provides proven solutions and services for telecom operators, infrastructure installers (towers) and related companies.

PUBLISHING

With the reduction in circulation and advertising revenue, publishers are looking for new ways to reduce costs. SCA can help reduce logistics and transportation costs while maintaining your service level. Our consultants work with you to identify areas for improvement. Our global network account managers, resources and facilities combine to define and implement management solutions to drive change and improve your processes.

DEFENCE

Our professional global end-to-end supply chain services provide innovative logistics solutions with a focus on program best practices, safety and security. SCA implements customised solutions to offer inbound and outbound logistics, scalable and flexible warehousing services, comply with HM customs and fully manage the UK and international transportation.

PHARMACEUTICAL AND COLD CHAIN

SCA's pharmaceutical and cold chain consulting solutions meet the unique needs of the industry. We provide a full range of services, including storage in multiple temperature zones, cold chain solutions, reverse logistics, and advanced tracking technologies to ensure the entire supply chain And supply chain visibility-reports on the movement of goods in all modes of transportation.
